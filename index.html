Vanitha Swaminathan, Associate Dean for Research and Strategic Initiatives and Thomas Marshall Professor of Marketing at the University of Pittsburgh School of Business, is releasing a groundbreaking book this October titled Hyper-Digital Marketing: Six Pillars of Strategic Brand Marketing in an AI-Powered World.

Rapid changes in technology and the rise of artificial intelligence (AI) have ushered in a new era of hyper-digitalization. This transformation impacts how organizations operate, redefining traditional marketing roles and creating new ones that demand cross-functional collaboration.

Join us for an hour-long webinar exploring this phenomenon and the book’s key insights. Learn how marketers and technologists can navigate the sweeping changes of the AI age using a framework for strategic brand thinking designed for the workplace of tomorrow.
